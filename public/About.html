About the Project

Our Mission

The project aims to transform the way communities handle waste by making recycling effortless, rewarding, and part of everyday life. Our primary environmental goal is to reduce mixed waste sent to landfills and increase the rate of properly sorted recyclable materials to 30% within three years in our coverage areas.

What Inspired the Idea

The concept originated from a clear gap in the market:

People want to recycle, but there is no organized, convenient, or incentivized system that makes it easy. Instead of depending on public bins or informal collectors, the project digitizes the entire process through an on-demand collection model powered by a mobile app.

Our Approach

We combine technology, logistics, and incentives into one seamless ecosystem:

  • A mobile app for scheduling pickups, tracking points, and redeeming rewards.
  • A professional collection team equipped with dedicated recycling trucks.
  • Smart routing algorithms to ensure fast, efficient, and cost-effective operations.
  • A transparent points system that converts every kilogram into instant rewards.

Environmental + Economic + Social Impact

Our project is built on three pillars:

Environmental:

Reducing mixed waste, lowering pollution levels, and promoting responsible sorting from the source.

Economic:

Selling clean, pre-sorted materials (plastic, aluminum, paper) to recycling factories at higher market value, creating a sustainable and scalable business model.

Social:

Changing community behavior by rewarding positive environmental actions and encouraging families, students, and residential communities to sort their waste properly.
