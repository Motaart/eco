How the Points System Works

Our collection team visits your location and weighs your materials right in front of you using certified digital scales connected to the app.

Each material type is recorded separately — plastic, aluminum, and paper — ensuring accuracy and transparency.

The system instantly converts the recorded weight into points based on the value of each material (e.g., aluminum earns higher points per kilogram).

Your points appear immediately in your in-app wallet with no delays.

Use your points to unlock:

  • Discount vouchers
  • Store credit
  • Offers from partner brands

Partners

Why Partner With Us

We provide brands with a powerful opportunity to connect with eco-conscious users while generating real commercial value. By joining our network, partners benefit from increased customer traffic, enhanced brand image, and a direct role in supporting sustainable behavior.

How Partnership Works

Your store or service becomes available as a reward option inside the app.

Users redeem their collected points for discounts, vouchers, or store credit at your brand.

You gain new loyal customers driven by our incentive system.

We promote your brand across our app and awareness campaigns.

Partner Categories

We collaborate with a range of sectors, including:

  • Supermarkets & retail chains
  • Telecommunications companies
  • E-commerce stores
  • Recycling factories (core partners for materials processing)

Benefits for Partners

  • Increased customer acquisition through our reward system
  • Stronger brand perception as an environmentally responsible company
  • Zero operational burden — our team handles the entire collection and logistics
  • Transparent reporting on user engagement and voucher usage
